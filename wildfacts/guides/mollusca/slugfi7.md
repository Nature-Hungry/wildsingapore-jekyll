![](slugs/nudibranchia/gymnodoris/060102sisg3720m.jpg) [Yellow-spotted  
gymnodoris](slugs/nudibranchia/gymnodorisothers.htm) *Gymnodoris* sp.

![](slugs/nudibranchia/fidelis/220419sjijt2a.jpg)  
[Reliable   
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/fidelis.htm) *Goniobranchus fidelis*.

![](slugs/nudibranchia/rubropapulosa/050918smkg2720m.jpg) [Orange-spotted   
gymnodoris](slugs/nudibranchia/rubropapulosa.htm)  *Gymnodoris rubropapulosa*

1cm
or less. Body black with yellow spots. Seaweeds and seagrasses.
Sometimes seen on some of our Northern shores.
2-5cm.
Body white, yellow, orange with tiny yellow spots. Seagrass meadows
and seaweeds. Sometimes seen on our shores.
2-5cm.
Body broad white with undulating red edge pattern. Rhinophores and gills tipped in black. Coral rubble and reefs. Sometimes seen on our shores.
 
2-5cm.
Body white, large orange spots, rhinophores orange and gills edged
in orange. Coral rubble. Sometimes seen on our Southern shores.

![](slugs/nudibranchia/lineolata/050917smkg2532ma.jpg)  
[Lined   
chromodoris nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/lineolata.htm) *Chromodoris lineolata*

![](slugs/nudibranchia/hypselodoris/020629chgd1310m.jpg)  
[Pink-gilled hypselodoris   
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/hypink.htm) *Hypselodoris* sp.

![](slugs/nudibranchia/hykanga/011203cjcpd1880m.jpg)  
[Kanga hypselodoris   
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/hykanga.htm) *Hypselodoris kanga*

![](slugs/nudibranchia/hymaritima/060429skdg5808m.jpg)  
[Maritima hypselodoris  
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/hymaritima.htm) *Hypselodoris maritima*

2-3cm.
Body long with broad tail, black with fine white lines. Black line
along the inner edge of the orange border on the foot. Coral rubble.
Seasonally common on our Southern shores.
2-3cm.
Body long, narrow with a long tail. Pale with small blue and orange
spots, gills pink-edged without spots. Coral rubble. Sometimes seen
on our Northern shores.
2-3cm.
Body long, narrow with a long tail. Pale with small blue and orange
spots, blue bars, gill triangular cross-section with orange spots.
Coral rubble. Sometimes seen on our Northern shores.
2-3cm.
Body long, narrow with a long tail. Pale with black blotches, yellow
margin, pink-edged gills. Coral rubble. Sometimes seen on some of
our shores.

![](slugs/nudibranchia/tumulifera/080521chgtch01m.jpg)  
[Maroon cow  
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/tumulifera.htm) *Goniobranchus tumulifera*

![](slugs/nudibranchia/orientalis/240625bbtch3m.jpg)  
[Black cow  
nudibranch](slugs/nudibranchia/orientalis.htm) *Chromodoris orientalis*

2-3cm.
Body broad white with maroon spots.
Edge of body yellow with maroon spots, gills and rhinophores white.
Coral rubble. Sometimes seen on our Northern shores.
2-3cm.
Body broad white with black spots.
Edge of body orange no black spots, gills and rhinophores orange.
Silty shores. Sometimes seen on our Northern shores.
