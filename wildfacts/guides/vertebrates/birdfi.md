![](birds/albirostris/050126cjd9848m.jpg)  
[Oriental pied-hornbill](birds/albirostris.htm)  
*Anthracoceros albirostris*

![](birds/gallus/090906prpd0489m.jpg)  
[Red junglefowl](birds/gallus.htm)  
*Gallus gallus*

 
 
 
About
70cm. Large, long bill, black-and-white plumage. Coastal forest at
Pulau Ubin and Changi. Commonly seen.
Colourful
male (about 80cm) with white ear patches and a white puff at base
of tail. Drab female (about 40cm) dull brown streaky plumage with
white ear patches. Their feet lead-grey. Commonly seen at Pulau Ubin
and some coastal parks.
 
 
 
  
in
red are those listed among the threatened animals of Singapore
from Davison, G.W. H. and P. K. L. Ng and Ho Hua Chew, 2008. *The Singapore
Red Data Book: Threatened plants and animals of Singapore*.
