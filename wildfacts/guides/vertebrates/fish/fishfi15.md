6-8cm
to 22cm. Black diagonal bars, blue spots on face and sides, Sometimes
seen on our Northern mangroves.

8-10cm,
to 13cm. Scattered yellow spots, no black stripe along the body.
First dorsal fin rounded with black and white margin. Sometimes
seen on our Northern mangroves.

6-12cm.
Gold spots on 'cheeks' and underside of the body. Rocky shores,
among seagrasses. Commonly seen on many of our shores.

15-27cm.
Largest mudskipper, with black stripe along the side of the body.
Mangroves. Commonly seen on our Northern shores.

14cm.
Long slender body plain grey. Performs 'leaps' as courtship display. Sometimes seen on our Northern shores.
