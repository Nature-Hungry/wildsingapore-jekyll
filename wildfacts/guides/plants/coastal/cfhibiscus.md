****Plants
with hibiscus-like flowers on the shores  
How to tell them apart?**  
*updated
Aug 09***

---

These two plants
look very similar at first glance. Here's more on how to tell them
apart.

---

**[Sea
hibiscus](hibiscus/tiliaceus)***(Hibiscus
tiliceaus)*

![](hibiscus/tiliaceus/090117smkd5536m3.jpg)

![](../../insecta/dysdercus/081206smkd2478m3.jpg)

![](hibiscus/tiliaceus/090321krjo3211491m3a.jpg)

Maroon
stigma (the sticky tip   
of the columnar structure   
in the middle of the flower).

Fruit
smaller and splits when ripe.

Leaves
thinner   
with less obvious veins.

---

**[Baru-baru](thespesia/populnea)***(Thespesia
polpunea)*

![](thespesia/populnea/031019ubnd0029m3a.jpg)

![](thespesia/populnea/031019ubnd0005m3.jpg)

![](thespesia/populnea/090321krjo3211490m3a.jpg)

Yellow
stigma.

Fruit
larger and does not split.

Leaves
thicker, more leathery,   
with more obvious yellow veins.
