red are those listed among the threatened plants of Singapore from

Plants and Animals of Singapore.*  
  

**Northern
shores**

**ecp**:
east coast park, **tm**:
tanah merah, **prp:**pasir ris park,**chg:** changi,  **ubn:** pulau ubin (other than cj)**cj**: chek jawa, **skd**:
pulau sekudu, **bb**: beting bronok, **tus**:
tuas

**Southern
shores**

**lab**:
labrador + berlayar creek,  **snt**:
sentosa, **cyr**:
cyrene reef,  **kus**:
kusu island, **laz**:
lazarus island, **sji**:
st. john's island, **sis**:
sisters islands,  **jng:** pulau jong**, tkr:**pulau tekukor**,
hnt**: pulau hantu, **tpg**:
terumbus pempang darat, tengah and laut, **smk**:
pulau semakau,  **tsm**: submerged reefs off semakau - terumbu raya, beting
bemban besar, terumbu bemban, terumbu semakau, **rfl**: raffles lighthouse, **lfa:**
live firing area (Salu, Sudong, Pawai, Senang, Biola and submerged
reefs in the area)
