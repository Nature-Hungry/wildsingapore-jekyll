**Fan-shaped
green seaweeds compared  
How to tell them apart?  
*updated

These fan-shaped
seaweeds are sometimes confused for one another. Here's more on how
to tell them apart.

**[Ruffled
fan green seaweed](avrerecta)**  
*Udotea* sp.

One
paddle-shaped blade flat not ruffled, thick rather stiff, sometimes
divided into four or more 'wings'.

Paddle-shaped
blade flat usually with ruffled margins, thick rather stiff, usually
in clusters of a few blades.

**[Pleated
fan green seaweed](rhipidosiphon)** *Rhipidosiphon javensis*

Circular
blade flat not ruffled, thick rather stiff, usually in clusters
of many blades.

Fan-shaped
blade flat, thin and rather floppy when out of water, edges not
ruffled although the blade amy be folded, usually in clusters of
many blades.
