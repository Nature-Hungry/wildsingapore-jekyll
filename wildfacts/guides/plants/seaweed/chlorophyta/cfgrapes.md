tell apart these two green seaweeds with little round shapes.

sea grapes](lentillifera)**

*Caulerpa racemosa*

is a distinct constriction where   
the round 'grape' connects to the stem.

The
'grape' may be round or club-shaped.

The bunch is usually sausage-shaped with the round 'grapes' tightly
