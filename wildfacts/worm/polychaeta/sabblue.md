**Where
seen?** This fanworm is sometimes seen in sandy areas, even in silty sand, on our
Northern shores.  
  
**Features:** Fan 6-8cm in diameter.
The fan banded bluish, brown and white sometimes with portions in
bright yellow. Overall shade may be purplish-blue or greenish. Usually
found alone in sand.  
  
Sometimes this fanworm is seen at low tide with the fan still out
of the tube and the fan 'feathers' stuck together into 'petals' so
that it looks like a bizarre flower!
