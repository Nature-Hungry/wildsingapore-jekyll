in
red are those listed among the threatened animals of Singapore from
Davison, G.W. H. and P. K. L. Ng and Ho Hua Chew, 2008. *The Singapore
Red Data Book: Threatened plants and animals of Singapore*.  
  
*Location in
italics indicates sightings shared by others.  
  
Species are difficult to positively identify without close examination.  
On this website, they are grouped by external features for convenience of
display.*

**Northern
shores**

**pgl:**  punggol, **prp:** pasir ris park, **chg:** changi, **cj**: chek jawa including pulau ubin, **skd**:
pulau sekudu,  **bb**: beting bronok,   
**tus**:
tuas, **ecp**:
east coast park, **tm**:
tanah merah.

**Southern
shores**

**lab**:
labrador + berlayar creek,  **snt**:
sentosa, **cyr**:
cyrene reef,  **kus**:
kusu island, **laz**:
lazarus island, **sji**:
st. john's island, **sis**:
sisters islands,  **jng:** pulau jong**, tkr:**pulau tekukor**,
hnt**: pulau hantu, **tpg**:
terumbus pempang darat, tengah and laut, **smk**:
pulau semakau,  **tsm**: submerged reefs off semakau - terumbu raya, beting
bemban besar, terumbu bemban, terumbu semakau, **rfl**: raffles lighthouse, **lfa:**
