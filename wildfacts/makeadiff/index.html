As an Individual

You can make a difference too. The more people know and understand our local biodiversity, there'll be a bigger voice for them. This increases the likelihood they can get conserved if they are conservationally significant!

If you would like to know what happens in the entire process of a development project, please refer to Guide to the EIA process.
