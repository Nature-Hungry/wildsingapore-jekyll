**Snake,
eel or eel-like fish?  
How to tell them apart?***updated Sep 2020*

---

 **Long
and squirmy** These kinds of animals are often mistaken for
one another.

**[Yellow-lipped
sea snake](../vertebrates/snakes/hydrophiidae/laticauda)** *Laticauda
colubrina*The
sea snake is a reptile.
No
gills or gill openings.   
No
tube on the nostrils.   
No
fins.   
Has
large scales.
The tail is flattened and paddle-like.

---

![](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/tile/101224sisd4114m3.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/tile/080621labd6014m3c.jpg)  
![](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/tile/101224sisd4114m3a.jpg)
**[Moray
eel](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/muraenidae)** Family
MuraenidaeThe moray eel is a fish   
and a true eel.
Gill
openings reduced to pores.  
Pair
of tubular nostrils at the snout.  
No
pectoral fins.   
No
scales.
Dorsal and anal fins are continuous with the tail fin.

![](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/evermann/081231sisd4444m3.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/evermann/081231sisd4439.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/evermann/081231sisd4442m3.jpg)
**[Snake-eel](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/ophichthidae)**   
Family OphichthidaeThe snake-eel is a fish,  
but a true eel.   
Gill
openings reduced to slits.  
Pair
of tubular nostrils at the snout.  
Has pectoral fins.   
No
scales.
Tail tip bony and stiff, sharply pointed.

---

![](../vertebrates/fish/pseudochromidae/subducens/040703sisg0982m3.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/pseudochromidae/subducens/040703sisg0982m3a.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/pseudochromidae/subducens/040703sisd1598m3a.jpg)  
[**Carpet
eel-blenny**](../vertebrates/fish/pseudochromidae/subducens)   
*Congrogadus subducens*  
Family Pseudochromidae  
The
carpet eel-blenny is a fish,  
but NOT a true eel.
Has
large gill covers.  
Has large eyes.   
No
tube on the nostrils.  
Has
pectoral fins.   
Has
small scales.
Dorsal and anal fins are continuous with the tail fin.

---

![](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/canius/050818cftg1436m3.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/canius/050818cftg1436m3a.jpg)
![](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/canius/050818cftg1436m3b.jpg)
**[Eeltail catfish](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/plotosidae)**  
Family Plotosidae  
The
eeltail catfish is a fish  
but NOT a true eel.
Has 'whiskers' (barbels).  
Gill openings reduced.   
No
tube on the nostrils.  
Has
pectoral fins.   
No scales.
Dorsal and anal fins are continuous with the tail fin.

comparisons** 

---

![](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/wormeel/040817sntd4718m3.jpg)  
The [worm-eel](../vertebrates/fish/ophichthidae/wormeel)
is a fish,  
but NOT a true eel.

![](../vertebrates/fish/bythitidae/iluocoeteoides/060531labd2675m3.jpg)  
The [cuskeel](../vertebrates/fish/bythitidae/iluocoeteoides) is a fish,   
but NOT a true eel.

**![](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/lineatus/040919sntd7020m3.jpg)**  
[Striped eeltail
catfishes](../vertebrates/fish/plotosidae/lineatus)   
also have eel-like tails.

---

![](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/reevesii/100527tmd3450m3.jpg)  
The [moray eel](../vertebrates/fish/muraenidae/muraenidae) is a fish

![](../vertebrates/snakes/cerberus/031128sbwrad0089m3.jpg)  
[Dog-faced
water snake](../vertebrates/snakes/colubridae/cerberus)

![](../vertebrates/fish/syngnathidae/seagrass/071001chgd6784m3.jpg)  
The [pipefish](../vertebrates/fish/syngnathidae/pipefish) is a fish.  
More on [stick-like fishes](../vertebrates/fish/cfstick).

---

![](../worm/nemertea/verylong/050325sjid1973m3.jpg)  
[Ribbon worms](../worm/nemertea/nemertea) can be
very long.   
They usually stay on the ground   
and don't swim about very fast.

![](../echinodermata/holothuroidea/leucospilota/051105sisd6316m3.jpg)  
[Long black
sea cucumbers](../echinodermata/holothuroidea/leucospilota)   
don't move much.

![](../cnidaria/others/gorgonacea/whip/030714bbd0067m3.jpg)  
A [sea whip](../cnidaria/others/gorgonacea/gorgonacea)
is a colony of tiny animals that are usually anchored to the bottom,
although the entire whip-like colony may sway in the currents.
