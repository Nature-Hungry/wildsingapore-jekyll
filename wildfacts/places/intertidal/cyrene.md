**Conservation
status:** Does not appear on the [URA
Master Plan 2008](http://www.ura.gov.sg/uramaps/?config=config_preopen.xml&preopen=Master%20Plan) or the [Parks
and Waterbodies Plan](http://www.ura.gov.sg/uramaps/?preopen=Special%20and%20Detailed%20Controls%20Plans%20%28SDCP%29).   
**Current conservation activities:**

- Since 1986,
  Cyrene Reef is one of the survey sites of [underwater
  coral reef surveys](http://coralreef.nus.edu.sg/survey/data.htm) by [ReefFriends](http://www.bluewatervolunteers.org/reeffriends/)
  of [NParks](http://www.nparks.gov.sg/cms/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=120&Itemid=123)
  and the [Blue Water
  Volunteers](http://www.bluewatervolunteers.org/). The intertidal area is also regularly surveyed
  by [wildsingapore](https://images.naturehungry.sg/).
- 2006-now:
  [TeamSeagrass](http://teamseagrass.blogspot.sg/) with
  [NParks](http://www.nparks.gov.sg/cms/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=120&Itemid=123)
  has been monitoring [seagrasses
  at Cyrene](http://www.seagrasswatch.org/Singapore.html).
- Ad-hoc: Short-term
  studies of seagrasses, Common sea stars (*Archaster typicus*)
  and Knobbly sea stars (*Protoreaster nodosus*).
- Ongoing:
  An effort to bring decision makers in government and corporations[to visit Cyrene](http://cyrenereef.blogspot.sg/).

**About
the name:** *Terumbu*=A reef or rock at
low tide but submerged at high water; *Pandan* probably
refers to Sungei Pandan, a river on the mainland that
lies north of these reefs. Greek mythology describe Cyrene
as an 'extraordinarily beautiful' woman descended from
a river god and a nymph.
