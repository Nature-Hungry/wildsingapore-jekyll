****Sea
almond and similar trees on the shores  
How to tell them apart?**  
*updated
Aug 09***

---

These plants
look very similar at first glance. Here's more on how to tell them
apart.

---

[**Sea
almond**](terminalia/catappa)  
(*Terminalia catappa*)

![](terminalia/catappa/040831sjid6040m3.jpg)

*![](terminalia/catappa/090926cjd1967m3.jpg)*

![](terminalia/catappa/090125kntd6090m3.jpg)

Large
leaves

Flowers
tiny,   
arranged on long stalks

Fruits
almond-shaped, small.

---

[**Ketapang
pasir**](guettarda/speciosa)   
(*Guettarda speciosa*)

![](guettarda/speciosa/090405smkd9081m3.jpg)

![](guettarda/speciosa/090405smkd9085m3.jpg)

![](guettarda/speciosa/090405smkd9077m3a.jpg)

Large
leaves.

Flowers
trumpet-shaped, small.

Fruits
round, small.

---

**[Putat
laut](barringtonia/asiatica) or Sea poison**   
(*Barringtonia asiatica*)

![](barringtonia/asiatica/020120sbwrcp5221m3a.jpg)

![](barringtonia/asiatica/030910sbwrad0078m3.jpg)

![](barringtonia/asiatica/090410ubnd9435m3a.jpg)

Large
leaves.

Flowers
large, fluffy.

Fruits
squarish, large.
