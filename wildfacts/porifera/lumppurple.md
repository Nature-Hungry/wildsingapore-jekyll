**Lumpy
purple sponge  
*Haliclona* sp.\*  
Family
Chalinidae***updated Oct 2016*

---

**Where
seen?** This purple sponge is sometimes seen on our Northern shores, growing
on coral rubble. **Features:** 10-15cm.
The sponge is encrusting with knobs with large holes at the tips.
Colour purple.
