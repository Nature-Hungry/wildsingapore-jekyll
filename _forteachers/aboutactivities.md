## How to Submit an Activity

We're looking for activities that:
- Have clear learning objectives and outcomes
- Include detailed instructions
- List all required materials
- Include safety considerations
- Allow students to connect with Singapore's biodiversity

**Format required:**
- Microsoft Word or Google Document
- Have names of authors, date written, amount of participants, and subject listed.

**Email your activity to:** [thewildsingapore@gmail.com](mailto:thewildsingapore@gmail.com)

---

## Tips for Teachers Using These Activities

- **Adapt to your context:** All activities can be modified for your specific location and student level
- **Plan ahead:** Read the entire activity before class and gather materials
- **Pre-visit:** If the activity involves fieldwork, visit the location beforehand
- **Safety first:** Always brief students on safety protocols
- **Engage with the content:** Your enthusiasm is contagious!
- **Connect to curriculum:** Link activities to official learning objectives
- **Document learning:** Take photos/videos of your students in action
