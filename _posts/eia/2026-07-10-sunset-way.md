## Main Issues

### Ecological Connectivity within the Site

The Proposed Retained Area (PRA) mostly overlaps with the site's Area of High Ecological Value (AHEV) and CS plants, which is ideal. However, there is a small stretch of PRA at the southwestern part of the site which is too skinny to allow any sensitive fauna to travel from east to west of the PRA. The lack of buffers also means that the AHEV will be left to fend for itself against Forest Edge Effect, which means the AHEV can possibly deteriorate after development due to a change in conditions. Changes include decrease in humidity and increase in wind, which can harm native trees who have adapted to grow in humid rainforests.
