**Where
seen?** This tiny
fanworm is sometimes seen on some of our shores, usually in silty
sandy sheltered lagoons, often in small groups of 2-3 individuals near burrows.  
  
**Features:** On this page are included
small fanworms with spots found in sandy areas. Fan
about 1cm in diameter. The
small fan is a single spiral, white with brown portions that result
in a chevron banded pattern. Some are brownish with white spots. They are highly sensitive to disturbances and will disappear at the slightest
sign of danger.

 

  
*\***Species
are difficult to positively identify without close examination.   
On this website, they are grouped by external features for convenience
of display.*
