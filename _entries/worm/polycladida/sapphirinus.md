**Where seen?**
This beautiful worm is rarely seen.

**Features:**
About 4cm long. Body black to reddish brown, margin bright blue, the outer margin comprising black with a very fine white outermost margin. Underside uniformly black with a fine white margin. The body is usually not highly ruffled. It has a pair of pseudotentacles that are simple and made up of folded edges of the body.
