**Egg
blobs**  
 *updated Apr 2020*

---

if
you learn only 3 things about them ...  
![](../photos/bullet.gif) Egg blob contain living eggs or tiny animals. Don't disturb
them.  
![](../photos/bullet.gif) Egg strings are found on mud or sand. Watch your step.  
![](../photos/bullet.gif)Please
don't bring egg blobs home. Thousands of baby animals
may die.
  
**Where
seen?** These translucent blobs are sometimes seen on our mudflats and sandflats.
Seasonally, in large numbers.  
  
**Features:** About 10cm long. Translucent
with tiny eggs embedded in the mass. Usually attached to the group,
often teardrop or light-bulb shaped when submerged. Possible animals
responsible for laying these eggs include sea slugs and worms.
