## Where seen?
 This tiny jewel of a crab was seen once on Labrador.  
  
## Features
 Body width 1-2cm. Body
and claws bright red with bumps and ridges. Like other brightly coloured
Xanthid crabs, it is probably [poisonous](xanthidae).
