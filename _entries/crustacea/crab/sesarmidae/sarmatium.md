**Mound
crab**   
***Sarmatium germaini*****Family Sesarmidae***updated
Dec 2019*

---

**Where
seen?** This
spherical crab is seldom seen outside the [Mud
lobster mounds](../../othercrust/lobster/thalassina) where it lives.  
  
**Features:** Body width 3-8cm. It
has a round body and flat legs with pointed tips. The pincers are
stout and fat.  
  
**What does it eat?** It eats mangrove
leaves, dragging these into its burrow to eat them in peace and quiet.  
  
**Status and threats:** This crab
is listed as 'Endangered' in the Red List of threatened animals of
Singapore.
