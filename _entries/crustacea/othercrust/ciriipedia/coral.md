**Coral
barnacle** ***Pyrgoma* sp.\*  
Family Pyrgomatidae**  
*updated
Mar 2020*

---

**Where
seen?** These
tiny burrowing animals pock-mark some hard corals.  
  
**Features:** Often, all that can
be seen on the surface of the hard coral are tiny slits (0.2cm), sometimes
bright blue.
