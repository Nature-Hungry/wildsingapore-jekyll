## Where seen?
This small flat fish is commonly seen on Changi, in sandy areas near seagrasses.

**Features**
:
To about 9cm long, those seen 4-5cm. Eyes on right side. Small, highly curved mouth. Body oval and very flat. Tail fin joined to the dorsal and anal fins only at the base. Dark pectoral fin. Sand-coloured with speckles and spots.

**What does it eat?**
It hunts small animals that live on the sea bottom especially crustaceans.

**Human uses:**
Large specimens are economically important and sold fresh, frozen and dried-salted.
