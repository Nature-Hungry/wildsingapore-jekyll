**Grey
knight-goby**  
***Stigmatogobius sadanundio***  
**Family Gobiidae***updated
Sep 2020*

---

**Where
seen?** This large greyish goby with spots is sometimes seen in our mangrove
streams.  
  
**Features:** About 6cm long. Pearly
grey with 3-4 rows of small round black spots on the sides. Spots
and streaks also on the fins and tail.  
  
**What does it eat?** According to
FishBase, it feeds on small fishes and invertebrates, including mosquito
larvae.
