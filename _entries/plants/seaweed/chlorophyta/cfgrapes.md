tell apart these two green seaweeds with little round shapes.

sea grapes](lentillifera)**  
*Caulerpa lentillifera*

**[Oval
sea grapes](racemosa)**  
*Caulerpa racemosa*

[**Bell
sea grape seaweed**](bell)  
*Caulpera racemosa* var. *turbinata*
