if

![](../../../photos/bullet.gif)Egg capsules contain living eggs or tiny snails. Don't
disturb them.  
![](../../../photos/bullet.gif)
 **Where seen?** These rosette-like ribbons of eggs are sometimes
seen on many shores.  
  
**Features:** Diameter 8-1cm. Frilly
ribbon of eggs embedded in mucus, laid in a spiral on hard surfaces
or among seaweeds and seagrasses. Usually white or beige.
