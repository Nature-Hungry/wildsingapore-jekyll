**Smooth
cuttlefish *awaiting identification\**** **Family Sepiidae***updated
May 2020*

---

**Where
seen?** These delightful animals are sometimes commonly seen
on our Northern shores. Usually seen among seagrasses.   
  
**Features:** 8-13cm long. Body oval
with fins all around the edges, sometimes with glittering spots or
dashes. Short fat arms. Body texture generally smooth and does not
form into bumps or pimples.  
  
Cuttlefishes are difficult to distinguish in the field. The cuttlefishes
on this page may not all be of the same species.
