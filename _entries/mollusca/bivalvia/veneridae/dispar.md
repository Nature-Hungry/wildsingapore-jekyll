**Discrepant
venus clam**  
***Gafrarium dispar\******Family Veneridae***updated
Nov 11*

---

**Where
seen?** This clam is sometimes seen on sandy shores.
Elsewhere, they are found on intertidal shores with sand.   
  
**Features:** About
4-6cm. Shell thick with fine ribs and usually a pattern of fine broken
lines.
