## Where seen?
This bullet-shaped burrowing snail was seen on Changi and Chek Jawa. Initially in some numbers, then dwindling away.

## Features

2-5cm. Shell thick with fine ridges in a cross-hatched pattern. The shell has a dark 'skin' which can peel off. There is notch at the tip of the shell for the siphon to emerge. The animal is white with a broad foot and short tentacles. Operculum small and brown.

**What does it eat?**
Mitre snails are carnivorous and many feed on worms. They may also scavenge.
